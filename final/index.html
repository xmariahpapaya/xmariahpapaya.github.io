About

I grew up in Beverly, Massachusetts and currently reside in Boston. I attend Massachusetts College of Art and Design as a junior, working towards my BFA in graphic design. Although my main focus is graphic design and typography, I have a passion for creating both in 2D and 3D media. I love to illustrate (both digitally and by hand), paint, collage with painted paper, and sculpt—especially with glass and ceramics.
